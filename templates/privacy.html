01

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic session information to keep you logged in securely.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

02

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • Authenticate your account and maintain a secure session.
  • Improve performance and fix issues with the service.

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core service.

03

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

04

Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may use a limited set of trusted infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting, database) who process data strictly on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations.

We do not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or social tracking tools.

05

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. At any time you may:

  • Access and export the expense data stored in your account.
  • Update or correct your account information via profile settings.
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.
06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service evolves. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

07

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided within the app.

You can also review our Terms and Conditions for more details on how Spendly operates.
